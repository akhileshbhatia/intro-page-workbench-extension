Using Workbench Enhancer!

Save and Delete Your Queries

After you write the query and click on the query button, you will see your recently executed query in the sidebar on left hand side.
The queries are listed as per the date and the time that you executed them on.
Also,you can delete the queries by clicking in the delete icon beside each query.

Search through your queries

You can search through all your queries by simply typing in the search box present at the top.
